Decision Points

Decision points are the specific, recurring times during your study when the pJITAI algorithm decides which intervention option to deliver (like sending a message or a prompt). While the algorithm does not use the actual times of the decision points, the frequency of the decision points is important for your intervention design, and the pJITAI Toolbox will use this frequency to describe how your personalized intervention component will work in practice.
