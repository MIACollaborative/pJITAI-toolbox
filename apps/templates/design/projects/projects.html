What are intervention components?

Intervention components are the elements of your intervention. In this current version of the pJITAI Toolbox, we ask you to design one intervention component at a time. If you want to incorporate personalization in multiple intervention components in your study, you can design a personalizing JITAI for each component.


Example: HeartSteps Study

For example, in the HeartSteps study, the intervention components are:

  1. activity suggestions
  2. planning for the next day’s activities
In the HeartSteps demo, we demonstrate designing the personalization of the activity suggestions.


Suggestion: Start with HeartSteps

We suggest that if you are not familiar with our toolbox, you start by going through our HeartSteps demo (which can be accessed by clicking "Add New Project" and then "Add HeartSteps Demo" in the left-hand menu). If you cannot see a left-hand menu with an option to "Add New Project," you may see a menu-like icon in the top right corner of your screen. Click it to open the left-hand menu. When you’re ready to use this tool to develop personalizing JITAIs for your study, click "Add New Project" and then "Add a new intervention component."
